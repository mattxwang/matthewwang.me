I anticipate that tracking people down might be challenging. To disambiguate: in this current iteration of the will, all named people are UCLA alumni who overlapped with my time there; almost all are Computer Science alumni. The network should be a connected (and almost complete) graph.

In the extremely unlikely case that all named persons are unavailable, I simply request that all possible assets are either donated to a non-profit (of whoever is executing my will!), while the rest is safely disposed of.

In no circumstance should my parents or any familial (i.e. non-spousal) relatives be contacted or be a beneficiary.
